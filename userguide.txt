Welcome to the my simple calculator app!

Basic Operations:
Add: Add two numbers together.
Subtract: Subtract one number from another.
Multiply: Multiply two numbers.
Divide: Divide one number by another (Note: Division by zero is not allowed).
How to Use the Calculator:
Open the application in your web browser.
You will be prompted to select an operation from the following:
Add
Subtract
Multiply
Divide
Enter the two numbers you want to perform the operation on.
Press the "Calculate" button.
The result of the calculation will be displayed.
Key Features:
Simple and user-friendly interface.
Provides immediate results.
Handles basic arithmetic operations.
